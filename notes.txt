Pins and stuff.

From the RFID boards:

"Blue bunch": SPI stuff

Purple: MISO -> 50
Green: MOSI -> 51
Yellow: SCK -> 52

"White bunch": Chip select pins

Brown: reader0 -> 46
Yellow: reader1 -> 47
Blue: reader2 -> 48
Green: reader3 -> 49

"Black bunch": Misc stuff

Brown: reset -> 44
White: irq -> 45 (not actually used

Others: power

Red-orange: 5V (via 3.3V regulator)
Black: ground
